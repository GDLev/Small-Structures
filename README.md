# Small Structures

Bring more life and storytelling to your Minecraft world with a set of carefully designed, **vanilla-friendly structures** that blend naturally into the environment. This datapack/mod adds small, immersive points of interest that feel like they’ve always belonged in the game.

## 🌡️ Biome Variants

Each structure comes in **three distinct variants**, adapting to different environments:

- 🌿 **Regular** – Designed for temperate biomes  
- ☀️ **Warm** – Adjusted for deserts and warm climates  
- ❄️ **Cold** – Modified to fit snowy and icy regions  

These variations ensure that every structure feels natural and immersive, no matter where it appears.

---

## 🎯 Design Philosophy

- ✅ **Vanilla style** – Built using only vanilla blocks and aesthetics  
- 🌱 **World-friendly** – Structures are small and non-intrusive  
- 🧭 **Exploration-focused** – Adds flavor without overwhelming gameplay  

---

## 📦 Compatibility

- Works with most world generation mods and datapacks  
- Designed to integrate seamlessly into existing worlds *(new chunks required for generation)*  

---

## 🚀 Why use this pack?

If you enjoy subtle world-building and immersive exploration, this pack enhances your Minecraft experience without changing core gameplay. Every structure tells a small story — it’s up to you to discover it.

*Make your world feel more alive, one structure at a time.*
